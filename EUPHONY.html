EUPHONY

(Western Solo Singing Competition)

RULES & REGULATIONS:-

1. Only songs of English language are allowed.
2. The vocalist/accompanist (if any) must be visible singing/playing in the video submitted.
3. Upload the file on google drive with the name “(NAAD ID)-Euphony” for eg. “006-Euphony” where 006 is the NAAD ID of the participant and then share the link on the portal.
4. The decision of the judges shall be final and binding.
5. General Guidelines:
  a. Participants can use their own track or a tanpura as per their convenience.
  b. Participants can either perform solo or can bring up to 2 accompanists for the competition but the judgement will be based only on vocal
  performance.
  c. Obscenity (at the discretion of judges) of any kind in the lyrical content of the song is not allowed and will lead to immediate disqualification.
  d. Autotune/Pitch correction is strictly not allowed.
  e. Video duration should be 3-5 minutes.
