Raageshree

(Classical Singing Competition)

Witness the elating spectacle of Raagas and lose yourselves in the prosperous heritage of Indian classical music with Raageshree- the Classical Solo Singing competition!

RULES:-
1. Only pure classical compositions are allowed.
2. Participants must perform solo or with a maximum of 2 accompanists can bring up to 2 accompanists for the competition but the judgement will be based only on the vocal performance.
3. No accompanying instruments are allowed except a harmonium, tanpura and tabla. Participants may bring their own instruments. A harmonium, electronic tanpura, electronic tabla and a tabla set will be provided.
4. Time limit is 10 minutes (Stage in to Stage out) and it should be strictly followed. Participants will be heavily penalized by the judges for not doing so.
5. The time limit will be extended in case of any technical inconveniences from the organizing side.
6. The decision of the judges shall be final and binding.

Contact: Arunjeet (9006812852), Sudakshina (8794547100)

Dhun

(Instrumental Competition)

The mellifluous notations of the keyboard, the divine strings of the sitar or the rocking rhythm of the guitar- immerse yourselves in the aura of symphony as NAAD presents to you the instrumental competition- Dhun!

RULES:-
1. All kinds of instruments (including beatboxing) are allowed.
2. Pure instrumental performance pertaining to any genre is allowed.
3. The participant must be visible playing in the video submitted.
4. Upload the file on google drive with the name “ -Dhun” for eg. “006-Dhun” where 006 is the NAAD ID of the participant and then share the link on the portal.
5. The decision of the judges shall be final and binding.
6. General Guidelines:
a. Participants cannot use a backing track however they can use a tanpura as per their convenience.
b. Participants can either perform solo or can bring up to 2 accompanists for the competition but the judgement will be based only on the main instrumentalist performance.
c. Video duration should be 3-5 minutes.

Contact: Devyani (6393568636), Rajarshi (7209545708)

Alankaar

(Eastern Music Competition)

With the peppy bollywood music, the soulful hindi songs or gazals, thumri, witness the splendour of symphonies in Hindi music with Alankaar- the Eastern Singing competition!

RULES:-
1. Only songs of Hindi language including Bollywood, Semi-Classical songs are allowed. Folk or Pure Classical (Hindustani/Carnatic) compositions are not allowed.
2. The vocalist/accompanist (if any) must be visible singing/playing in the video submitted.
3. Upload the file on google drive with the name “ -Alankaar” for eg. “006-Alankaar” where 006 is the NAAD ID of the participant and then share the link on the portal.
4. The decision of the judges shall be final and binding.
5. General Guidelines:
a. Participants can use their own track or a tanpura as per their convenience.
b. Participants can either perform solo or can bring up to 2 accompanists for the competition but the judgement will be based only on vocal performance.
c. Obscenity (at the discretion of judges) of any kind in the lyrical content of the song is not allowed and will lead to immediate disqualification.
d. Autotune/Pitch correction is strictly not allowed.
e. Video duration should be 3-5 minutes.
